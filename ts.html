Who is Sylvia?

Hey there! Meet Sylvia, your introverted guide. Sylvia struggled to understand what her path was in life. With her introvertedness, she believed she had a weakness and not a strength. Later learning about their strengths and discovered amazing things about herself in her own quiet space. She found out she's really good at some stuff, and she's super passionate about it too! She believes that everyone has their own special talents, kind of like superpowers. She's great at listening and understanding people, and she's seen how powerful it is when people and strength really connect. Sylvia's has had cool jobs, from working in airplanes and traveling the world, to helping out at companies recruiting and even giving advice and facilitating career development session for young people. She has seen herself use her strengths to grow. And guess what? She loves sports and staying active too! Her big idea is that we've all got something amazing inside us, just waiting to be discovered. Whether it's tackling work stuff, being awesome with family, or growing as a person, Sylvia thinks we've all got what it takes.
